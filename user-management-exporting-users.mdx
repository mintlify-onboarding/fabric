### Overview

You can export the list of users associated with your organizationâ€™s fabric account into a CSV file. 

### Procedure

1. In the left menu, click **Settings > Account Settings**.
2. Click **User Management**.
3. Click **Export Users**.  
A message prompt is displayed to confirm the action.
4. Click **Yes, Export**.

The CSV file is downloaded.
