### Overview

This document covers the process of editing and deleting collections.

### Prerequisites

Ensure you have created at least one collection.

#### Editing a Collection

1. In the left menu, click **Products > Attributes > Collections**.  
The **Collections** page is displayed.
2. Click a collection.  
The collection detail page is shown.
3. Mouse over the collection name and click the vertical ellipses (**⋮**).
4. Click **Manage collection**.  
The **Manage collection** menu is displayed.
5. Edit the collection as required.
6. Click **Save**.

The collection is saved.

#### Deleting a Collection

1. In the left menu, click **Products > Attributes > Collections**.  
The **Collections** page is displayed.
2. Click a collection.  
The collection detail page is shown.
3. Click the vertical ellipses (**⋮**) to the right of the **ROOT** title.
4. Click **Delete collection**.  
The **Delete Collection** popup is displayed.
5. Click **Delete**.

The collection is deleted.
