---
title: "Viewing Product Attribute Import History"
---

### Overview

You can check the status of bulk attribute import operations on the **Import History** page.

Successful imports are marked as **Completed**. Imports that failed are marked with **Error**.

### Prerequisites

Ensure you have imported at least one CSV file of product attributes.

### Procedure

1. In the left menu, click **Products > Attributes > Product**.  
The **Product Attributes** page is displayed.
2. Click **Import History**.  
The **Import History** page is displayed.
3. (Optional) Click the name of a CSV import.  
The import details page is displayed.
4. (Optional) Click **Download original file**.  
The CSV file is downloaded.
