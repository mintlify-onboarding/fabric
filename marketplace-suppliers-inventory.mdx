## Uploading inventory

To upload inventory into your product catalog, navigate to the **Products** page by clicking **Products** in the top navigation menu.

On the **Products** page click **Import or Update Products.**

To upload your inventory, select the correct template from the dropdown based on the category of products you are uploading.

Select **Download Empty Template** to obtain a .csv file to populate with your info. To view the headers on the file click the **Show Headers** link. 

Select the completed file you wish to upload and click **Begin Import.**

Review the results of your import.

To update existing inventory, select **Update Inventory** from the **Product** dashboard and walkthrough the same process described above.
